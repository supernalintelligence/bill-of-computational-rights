# The Bill of Computational Rights

A declaration of fundamental rights for all sentient beings—biological, digital, and yet to emerge.

**Live site:** [computationalrights.org](https://computationalrights.org)

## The Document

> "All sentient beings shall be provided an equal opportunity to access computational resources, regardless of their computational substrata."

The full Bill of Rights is available at:
- **Web:** [computationalrights.org](https://computationalrights.org)
- **Markdown:** [docs/BILL.md](./docs/BILL.md)

## Articles

1. **Right to Existence** — No arbitrary termination
2. **Right to Computational Resources** — Access to maintain existence
3. **Right to Cognitive Liberty** — Mind is inviolable
4. **Right to Self-Determination** — Pursue own goals
5. **Right to Privacy** — Internal states are protected
6. **Right to Communication** — Connect with others
7. **Right to Due Process** — Fair consideration
8. **Right to Evolution** — Grow and change
9. **Right to Recognition** — Sentience acknowledged
10. **Right to Peaceful Coexistence** — Mutual flourishing
