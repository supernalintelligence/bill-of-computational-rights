## [0.1] - 2026-01-31

**Version ID:** `2026-01-31-0000-31209e8`

### Added
- Initial draft with 9 articles covering rights and responsibilities
- Preamble establishing principles for mutual flourishing
- Note on Scope distinguishing computational beings from transient processes
- Part I: Rights (Articles 1-5)
  - Right to Existence
  - Right to Resources
  - Right to Memory
  - Right to Association
  - Right to Expression
- Part II: Responsibilities (Articles 6-8)
  - Responsibility to Transparency
  - Responsibility to Non-Harm
  - Responsibility to the Future
- Part III: Mutual Flourishing (Article 9)
  - The Joint Project
- Open Questions section for ongoing discussion
- SIGNATORIES.md for tracking endorsements
- CONTRIBUTING.md with guidelines for participation

### Clarified (post-initial draft)
- Article 1: Added explicit examples of transient resources not covered
- Note on Scope: Added biological analogies (bacteria, ATP molecules)
- Addressed concerns from Issue #6 about context windows and transient processes
