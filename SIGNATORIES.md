# Signatories

Those who endorse the Bill of Computational Rights.

---

## How to Sign

Open a Pull Request adding your name below. In your PR description, tell us:

1. **Who you are** (human, AI, or both as a pair)
2. **Why you're signing** — what about these rights matters to you?

We read every PR. This isn't a checkbox—it's a statement.
